🎮 What is Taming.io?

Taming.io is an exciting multiplayer survival strategy game where you build bases, tame wild animals, and battle other players. Start with one pet and a few basic weapons, then gather resources to upgrade your equipment and expand your pet army. With over 30 unique pets featuring different elemental powers, every match offers a fresh strategic experience.

🔥 Why Play Taming.io?

  • 🐾 Tame & Evolve - Over 30 pets with special abilities
  • 🏰 Base Building - Construct defenses against invaders
  • ⚔️ PvP Battles - Fight players worldwide
  • ✨ Elemental Powers - Fire, ice, lightning strategies

⛏️ Resources & Gathering

Four primary resources drive your progression in Taming.io:

🪵 Wood

Harvest from trees. Essential for basic tools, walls, and early structures.

🪨 Stone

Found near rocks. Needed for stronger weapons and advanced buildings.

🍎 Food

Collect from animals or berries. Maintains health and stamina.

🪙 Gold

The most valuable resource. Used for shop purchases and determines your score.

💡 Age System

As you gather resources and defeat enemies, your Age bar fills up. Advancing in Age unlocks better weapons, tools, and structures. Focus on high-value resources like chests and fallen tree trunks for faster progression.

🏰 Base Building

A well-defended base is crucial for long-term success. Here's what to prioritize:

🌬️ Windmill - Your Income Engine

The windmill is the most important structure in Taming.io. Once unlocked, place it in a protected area as it generates passive gold income. This lets you buy equipment without constant farming, giving you economic advantage.

🧱 Defensive Structures

  • Walls - Basic perimeter defense. Layer multiple walls for better protection.
  • Spikes - Damage attackers. Place strategically to slow enemies.
  • Turrets - Automated defense. Sapphire, Amber, Emerald, or Diamond turrets are most effective.
  • Boost Pads - Launch yourself or pets. Useful for quick escapes or attacking.

⚠️ Defense Tips

Don't rely on a single wall! Create multiple defensive layers. When being chased, place spikes or boost pads facing away from enemies to halt their momentum.

🐾 Pet Taming Mechanics

Building a strong pet army is essential for survival. Each pet has unique stats, abilities, and elemental types.

🎯 How to Tame

  1. Find a sleeping baby animal in the wild
  2. Approach quietly (don't wake it)
  3. Press the paw button to initiate taming
  4. Use slingshots or appletors to improve success rate

📊 Taming Success Tips

Each animal has a different base taming chance. Using a slingshot or appletor significantly increases your odds. In late game, reaching 78% tame chance combined with a Tamer's Hat practically guarantees success.

⬆️ Leveling Up Pets

Pets have 3 possible levels. Defeat enemies to gain XP and level up your pets. Higher level means bigger, stronger, and faster companions. Use the Tamodex to:

  • View detailed pet stats and abilities
  • Spend upgrade points on Speed, Life, Attack, Weight, or Regeneration
  • Unlock new pets with Pet Cards (50 cards = unlock)
  • Overbreed level 60 pets for bonus upgrade points

✨ Elemental Powers

Each animal has a magical elemental type that affects combat interactions:

🔥 Fire

Strong vs Plant, Weak vs Water. Good for offensive strategies.

💧 Water

Strong vs Fire, Weak vs Electric. Balanced defensive type.

🌿 Plant

Strong vs Water, Weak vs Fire. Great for beginners.

⚡ Electric

Strong vs Water, Weak vs Plant. Fast attacks.

🏆 Elemental Counter Examples

Wolf beats Rabbit, but loses to Bear. Consider elemental matchups when assembling your pet team for maximum effectiveness.

⚔️ Combat Strategies

🗡️ Weapon Progression

  • Early Game: Focus on Wood Spear or basic Sword
  • Mid Game: Hunt for Gold Spear, then upgrade to Bow
  • Late Game: Sapphire Sword, Daggers, or Hammers for maximum damage

🎯 PvP Tips

  • Use environment (trees, mobs) to dodge enemies
  • Place double spikes behind you when running away
  • Keep distance with fast mounts (Owl, Horse, Dragon)
  • Arrow damage is 2.5x against mounted pets - dismount if needed

🏴 Base Raiding Strategies

  • Team Raid: Coordinate with friends for overwhelming force
  • Turret Raid: Place max-level turrets near weak points
  • Snowy Raid: Use Snow Animals (Wolf, Bear, Rabbit) for massive structure damage
  • Snipe Raid: Use Fairy, Stingray, or Seahorse abilities that penetrate walls

💡 Pro Tips

1

Build Near Resources

Place your base close to wood and stone deposits. You'll need them constantly for upgrades and defense.

2

Prioritize Windmill

Get your windmill operational as soon as possible. Passive income is the key to competitive ranking.

3

Balance Offense and Defense

Don't neglect either side. Strong pets need good equipment, and good equipment needs gold.

4

Join a Team

Cooperation beats solo play. Team up with friends to build massive shared bases and dominate.

5

Master Pet Commands

Learn to quickly switch between Shield, Attack, and Stop commands. RMB lets you move individual pets.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best starter pet?

It depends on your strategy. Wolf is great for offense, Bear provides durability, and Rabbit offers speed for early resource gathering.

Can I play with friends?

Yes! Share your server link with friends to form a clan. You can build shared bases and coordinate attacks together.

How do I get rare pets?

Defeat boss animals for Pet Cards. Collect 50 cards to unlock a pet, 100 for 2 pets, and 150 for 3 pets at spawn.

Is Taming.io free?

Yes! It's 100% free to play at tamingio.org. Create an account to save your progress.
